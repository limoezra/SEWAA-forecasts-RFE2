Categories of Reliability Cost-Loss Ratios CRPS Comparison

Categories of reliability calculated for Ensemble Logistic Regression (ELR) fine-tuning of IFS and cGAN to predict probabilities of exceedances across different rainfall thresholds (in mm/day) and lead times (in days).

Categories of reliability are calculated using test years 2021, 2023 and 2024 following the methodology detailed in Weisheimer and Palmer (2014).
